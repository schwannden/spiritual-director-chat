# An Unanswered Question

question what gets our attention we are bombarded with ideas invitations arguments
and enthusiasms that claim to make us better or happy or safe we are yelled at bartered with
urged and pushed all of it can't be true all of it can't be important but some of it must be
how do we distinguish between the central and peripheral where do we get an orientation in
this dizzying world of argumentation how do we find our way home through the blinding storm of
controversy how do we purchase a personal place to stand in the pushing and jostling crowd of people
who claim to tell us the truth of our lives what gets our attention the loudest voice the cleverest
slogan, the biggest promise?
